How It Works

You Order

Book a laundry collection using our Mobile App or through Website.

We Collect

Our Agent will come to your door step to collect your Items. (Free pick-up)

We Clean

We take dirt very seriously. Your Clothes receive our expert treatment.

We Deliver

Hey pal, open up! Your clothes are delivered on time and as fresh as daisy. Didn't we tell you laundry is so easy with Washapp? (Free Delivery)

Your clothing deserves the best—that’s why we only work with the best experienced work force and state of the art machinery to ensure your laundry is cared to the highest standard possible.

Our team has a background in setting up norms in modern day industry, so to say we’re pretty particular about our process is an understatement. We work with each new team member for weeks to ensure they meet all of our criteria (from washing and drying procedures to how they fold).

 

Laundry Process

Garments are picked up and the customer is given a receipt

Garments are tagged to avoid any lose of garment

Garments are sorted by fabric, stain or colour

Some garments undergo pre-wash treatment for tough stains

Garments are washed with water & special detergents

Garments are pressed usually with an industrial press carefully. In some cases, even hand presssed

Clean Garments are packed/hang and as good as new!

Clothes returned to Customer with corresponding receipt
